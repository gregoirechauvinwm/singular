Our methodology

Backed by science.

01

Personality & Attachment Intelligence

We use advanced generative AI to uncover nuanced personality traits and attachment patterns, translating them into meaningful compatibility insights for modern relationships.

02

Predicting Chemistry

By analyzing data from thousands of real dating outcomes, our models identify patterns that help predict who you're most likely to connect with—before you even meet.

03

Continuous Feedback Loop

Every date is followed by a guided debrief with your AI matchmaker, capturing signals of success and friction to continuously refine and improve your future matches.

04

Implicit Signals

Your interactions with AI reveal subtle behavioral and emotional cues. This rich, previously untapped data allows us to understand how you communicate and who you naturally align with.

Our journey

3 phases of research.

Phase 1 2023–25

Date data collection

Collection of outcome data from over 10,000 dates. Participants pre-screened and mutually confirmed prior to each encounter.

Completed
Phase 2 Ongoing

Human-assisted blind dates

Compatibility algorithm trained on Phase 1 data, now deployed to schedule blind dates between highest-compatibility candidates. All matches reviewed by human researchers.

Apply to phase 2 New York City only
Phase 3 2027

Public roll-out

Full deployment of matching protocol at scale, with no human intermediary.
